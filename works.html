Literary Works

A comprehensive bibliography spanning six decades of Odia literature

Short Stories Novels Literary Criticism & Essays Translations & Spiritual Collected Works Other Works
Over 55 published works across short stories, novels, literary criticism, essays, translations, and spiritual writings. His complete stories are published as Galpa Samagra and his collected works span 4 volumes.

With Known Publication Years

A collection showcasing his emerging mastery of satirical prose.

Srunantu Sarbe Amrutasya Putra

"Listen, All Children of Immortality" — stories drawing from Upanishadic wisdom.

Ganjei O' Gabesana

Stories exploring search and discovery in everyday human life.

1965

Andha Ratira Surya

"Sun of the Dark Night" — stories illuminating hope in times of darkness.

1979

Akasha Patala

"Heaven and Hell" — Winner of the Odisha Sahitya Academy Award.

c.1983

Abhisapta Gandharba

"The Cursed Gandharva" — Winner of both the Sarala Award (1983) and Sahitya Akademi Award (1984). Also translated into Hindi (1992).
