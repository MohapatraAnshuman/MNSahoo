Works Translated Internationally

Abhisapta Gandharba translated into Hindi (1992), and best stories into English (Sahitya Akademi, 2021).

📚

Biographical Study Published

Sahityasadhak Mohapatra Nilamani Sahoo — a critical and biographical study cementing his scholarly legacy.

📜

4-Volume Collected Works

Mohapatra Nilamani Sahoo Rachana Samagra — the definitive compendium preserving his output for future generations.

🏆

Son Awarded Padma Shri

Guruprasad Mohapatra posthumously awarded the Padma Shri in 2022 — a testament to the family's enduring legacy.

From the Odisha Sahitya Academy Award to the Utkal Ratna, each honour recognised not merely individual works but a lifetime spent illuminating the Odia literary tradition with unparalleled depth and humanity.
